The Deal Nobody
Thought Would Close.

A 3-person Google SRE hire had been stalled for months.
Every offer died at compensation. So I built a structure nobody had tried before.

🔍

The Problem

Three senior SREs from Google. Compensation asks that legal and comp had already said no to twice. Search going nowhere.

💡

The Solution

I originated a 4-year sign-on clawback structure that gave candidates the total compensation they needed while giving the company the retention protection it required.

✅

All three signed. Packages up to $500K base plus $200K sign-on plus RSUs. 100% team retention through the full build phase.

My Approach

How I Work.

Before I Source
Understand
  • Run a structured intake with every hiring manager before touching the market
  • Define what great actually looks like, not just what is written on the req
  • Research the talent landscape and competitive compensation before posting
  • Set honest expectations on timeline, availability, and tradeoffs upfront
  • Identify the real blockers before they become offer killers
While I Search
Stay Connected
  • Check in with both the candidate and hiring manager every few days throughout the process
  • Build outbound passive pipelines before inbound ever catches up
  • Use Boolean, GitHub, and LinkedIn Recruiter with precision not volume
  • Personalize every outreach based on actual research, not templates
  • Report weekly on pipeline health, conversion rates, and market data
After the Close
Follow Through
  • Walk every finalist through the offer before it lands in their inbox
  • Check in with both the new hire and hiring manager at week one
  • Follow up again at 30, 60, and 90 days to confirm the transition is on track
  • Surface any early concerns before they become retention problems
  • The search is not done until they stay
