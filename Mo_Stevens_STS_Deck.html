My Approach

How I Work.

Before I Source
Understand
  • Run a structured intake with every hiring manager before touching the market
  • Define what great actually looks like for this specific role and team
  • Research the talent landscape and compensation benchmarks before posting
  • Set honest expectations on timeline, availability, and tradeoffs upfront
  • Identify the real blockers before they become offer killers
While I Search
Stay Connected
  • Check in with both the candidate and hiring manager every few days throughout the process
  • Build outbound passive pipelines using LinkedIn Recruiter and Boolean search
  • Report weekly on pipeline health, conversion rates, and market data
  • Maintain rigorous ATS data integrity across all active searches at all times
  • Surface concerns early before they become surprises at the offer stage
After the Close
Follow Through
  • Walk every finalist through the offer before it lands in their inbox
  • Check in with both the new hire and hiring manager at week one
  • Follow up again at 30, 60, and 90 days to confirm the transition is on track
  • Surface any early concerns before they become retention problems
  • The search is not done until they stay
